About Unfavored Merit

This isn’t about being famous — it’s about being faithful. Unfavored Merit is a small creative project with a big aim.

Songs that sound like something you’d actually listen to — with lyrics that point you straight to the Gospel.

The mission is to bring the story of our salvation to people, drawing people closer to God and one another through the Word of God. If even one conversation gets started because of a shirt or a song, this work is worth it.
